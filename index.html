Hi, welcome! I am Zhaochen Su (苏肇辰), a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), co-advised by Prof. Junxian He and Prof. May Fung.

I am currently an Agent Research Intern at Kimi (Moonshot AI), contributing to Kimi K2 Thinking, K2.5, K2.6, and K2.7. Before that, I worked with the post-training team at MiniMax, contributing to MiniMax-M2, and was a research intern at Shanghai AI Laboratory.
